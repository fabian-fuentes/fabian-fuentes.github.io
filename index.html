01 · About

Where economics meets data.

I'm an economist with a Master's in Applied Economics from Tecnológico de Monterrey. My thesis evaluated the impact of decision support tools on human performance under deep uncertainty.

I specialize in econometric modeling, decision science and geospatial analysis for public policy. I led Mexico's official poverty measurement at CONEVAL and now collaborate with the World Bank on climate pathway simulations through the SISEPUEDE model.
